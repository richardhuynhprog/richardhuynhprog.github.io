A place that I have travelled to is Japan. It was exciting to learn about different cultures and see a new point of view on how society could be. One big difference is public transportation and rail systems, reaching speeds between 275-320 kilometers, and a reduction of cars and parking. I would recommend that everyone go to visit.

Near the bay is enormous replica of a giant mechanical machine. This mech suit is apart of a long series of japanese animation "Gundam" which began in the right before the 1980s and reflects the interconnectivity of art and science in pop culture. You cannot ride it.

Having dinner can be entertainment as well. There are many types of themed restaurants and cafes that are interesting and detailed in execution. One that I went to was a Pokemon themed restaurant for children where I got to shake Pikachu's hand, he has short arms.
